Content

The two day event offers hands-on workshops and speakers. Speakers challenge assumptions and inspire designers to imagine and embrace strategies to shape the future.

Audience

Primer attracts over 200 designers from across the country. Practitioners come eager to share their knowledge, learn from others, and hone their skills.

Organizers

Primer flows out of San Francisco Speculative Futures, a meetup community. We focus on work and practice in the fields of speculative and critical design, design fiction, strategic foresight. We amplify and examine all approaches for designing for the future.
