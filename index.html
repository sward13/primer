What is Primer?

Primer is a two day conference featuring talks and workshops from designers, strategists, and educators who are using speculative and futures design thinking as a tool to create strategies for addressing the evolving world and the emerging technologies and issues that are unfolding within it.

Who attends Primer?

Primer attracts over 200 designers and practitioners from across the world. Attendees are at the forefront of this emergent field of focus and come eager to share their knowledge, learn from others, and hone their skills.

Who is behind Primer?

Primer is a product of San Francisco based meetup community, Speculative Futures. We highlight advancements in the fields of speculative and critical design, design fiction, strategic foresight. Our goal is to examine and amplify all approaches for designing for the future in order to demonstrate the benefits of futures design methods and strategies to corporations, governments, and institutions.
